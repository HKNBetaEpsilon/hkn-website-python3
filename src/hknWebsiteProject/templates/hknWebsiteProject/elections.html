Election Nominations

Due to the pandemic, HKN-BE elections for this semester will take place fully online. There will be a question and answer meeting on Sunday, April 19th, and then a discussion meeting on Monday April 20th.

Elections are scheduled for Tuesday, April 21st and Wednesday, April 22nd at vote.umich.edu. Please review the elections guide to see each position's responsibilities as well as the thoughts of the current and former office holders.

Should you have any questions about elections, or wish to nominate yourself or another member, please don't hesitate to contact our Elections Chair, Michael Benson (mlbenson@umich.edu)
