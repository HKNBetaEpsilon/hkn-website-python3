Eta Kappa Nu

The Beta-Epsilon chapter of Eta Kappa Nu (HKN) is composed of the top electrical, computer, and computer science engineers at the University of Michigan. We serve our community by operating the fully student-run dB Cafe, holding community service projects around Ann Arbor, and orginizing community events to encourage collaboration amoung the students and faculty. In addition, we collabortate with companies to speak at our meetings, as well as, host tech talks for the general campus community. Lastly, we like to have fun! So we have plenty of game nights and trips to whirlyball and lasertag.
